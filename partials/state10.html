Volume Predictor

Perhaps you are set on a definite price, but need to know exactly how many units to sell to reach your net profit target. This can easily be calculated by assessing four different factors

  1. Desired net profit amount.
  2. Total fixed costs.
  3. Sales price per unit.
  4. Variable cost per unit.

Simply enter the numbers (with or without decimals) without symbols ($8.55 would simply be entered as 8.55) into the appropriate input box and click on Calculate or hit enter. The answer will then be rounded up or down as a whole number.
