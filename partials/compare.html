Final Comparison Tool

Once you have your final price ranges, the final step is to compare how your calcuated prices compare to those of your competitors. This final tool allows you to compare your final cost-plus based price, value-based price, an average between the two, and three prices of various competitors.

Simple enter your prices in whole numbers (with or without decimals) into the appropriate input field without any symbols (such as $ signs) and click on the Display button.
